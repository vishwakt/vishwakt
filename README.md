## 🧠 About Me

I architect and build **large-scale distributed systems from scratch.** The kind that move millions of transactions, serve thousands of locations, and run reliably under real-world load. My work spans the full stack, with deep roots in:

- 🏗 **Systems Architecture** — distributed systems, event-driven pipelines, multi-tenant platforms
- ☁ **Cloud-Native Engineering** — AWS Lambda, SAM, DynamoDB, serverless-first design
- 💳 **Payments & Fintech Infrastructure** — multi-processor orchestration, network tokenization, multi-tender flows
- 🔗 **API Platforms** — GraphQL federation, REST, schema-driven design
- 📈 **Quant & ML Systems** — algorithmic trading, financial data pipelines, strategy research

I've been the founding full-stack engineer who took a company from zero to Series A, and the consulting architect brought in to redesign systems at scale for Fortune-class retailers and enterprise platforms.
